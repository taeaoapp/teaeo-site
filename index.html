Taeao – The NCEA Navigator For Pasifika Futures

A mobile app that turns confusing NCEA rules into a clear, visual journey – for students, parents, and whānau. Track UE, plan subjects, and connect today’s choices to tomorrow’s pathways.

Designed with Pasifika learners, families, and educators in mind
Core Features

Clarity, culture, and pathways – all in one app

Taeao brings together UE tracking, subject planning, scholarship discovery, and a Parent / Whānau view so everyone can see the journey clearly and move together.

UE Tracker – Know if you’re on track, anytime

See your literacy, numeracy, approved subject credits, and endorsements in one clear dashboard. No more guesswork or end-of-year surprises – Taeao shows exactly what’s missing and what to do next.

Subject Mapper – Link subjects to degrees and careers

Choose subjects with confidence. Taeao maps NCEA subjects to university degrees and careers – especially in STEM – so students don’t accidentally close doors by dropping a key subject.

Scholarship Finder – Unlock hidden opportunities

Many Pasifika students qualify for scholarships they never hear about. Taeao brings them together in one place, filterable by ethnicity, region, interests, and future pathway.

Parent & Whānau View – Bring the village into the journey

Parents and whānau don’t need to be NCEA experts. Taeao translates progress into simple language and visuals, so families can support decisions together and celebrate milestones along the way.

Why Taeao matters

Bridging the gap between policy and real life

NCEA rules are technically online – but not in a way that feels human, simple, or grounded in Pasifika values. Taeao acts as a translation layer so students, whānau, and schools can see the same map and move together.
